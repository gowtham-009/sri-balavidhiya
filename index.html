About us

Established in 2014 in Arni, Tiruvannamalai, Shri Bala Vidhya Mandir Matriculation Higher Secondary School is a leading educational institution dedicated to providing exceptional education. Managed by the Pvt. Unaided sector and located in a rural area, our school caters to students from Grades 1 to 12, offering a co-educational environment and an attached pre-primary section. With English as the medium of instruction, we ensure a supportive learning atmosphere for all students. Our faculty consists of over 40 dedicated professionals who are committed to delivering the highest quality education, fostering academic excellence, and promoting holistic development. Operating without a shift system, we prioritize continuity in education, ensuring a seamless learning experience. Shri Bala Vidhya Mandir Matriculation Higher Secondary School stands as a beacon of quality education, dedicated to nurturing young minds and shaping future leaders.

School Rules and Regulations

Dear Students and Parents,

At Shri Bala Vidhya Mandir Matriculation Higher Secondary School, we uphold certain regulations to ensure a disciplined and conducive learning environment. Please adhere to the following guidelines:

Punctuality: School hours are from 9:00 AM to 12:45 PM and 1:25 PM to 3:45 PM. Attendance is compulsory on all working days (Monday to Friday), and pupils should arrive on time. Permission for leave should be sought only for valid reasons, and unnecessary absences are not permitted during school hours.

Language Usage: Inside the school campus, students should converse only in English to enhance their language skills.

Footwear: Students must have a pair of black and white shoes as part of their uniform.

Parental Involvement: Parents are required to sign their child's homework daily to ensure their involvement in their child's academic progress.
